About LifeLink

LifeLink is a modern blood donation management system connecting donors, hospitals, and blood banks in real-time to save lives. Our mission is to make blood donation accessible, efficient, and impactful for everyone involved.

  • Find and join blood drives easily
  • Respond to emergency blood requests
  • Track your donation history
  • Support your community
